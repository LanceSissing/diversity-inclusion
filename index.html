Importance of D&I

We live in a time of great change as outdated perspectives are increasingly questioned and broader more enlightened points of view take their place. As the world awakens to the intrinsic value of every human being the creation recognition of the importanc of creating cultures of Diversity & Inclusion continues to grow.

From entire countries to individual organisations, focusing on Diversity & Inclusion fosters openness, creativity and productive, fulfilled experiences. Diversity encompasses differences in political beliefs, race, culture, sexual orientation, religion, class or gender identity. Inclusion means ensuring everyone is fully engaged and involved, valued, respected, treated fairly, and integrated into the local culture. It involves empowering individual by recognising their unique talents as part of fostering an inclusive environment.

Both aspects of Diversity and Inclusion are equally iportant and they operate hand in hand with one another - diversity without inclusion can result in a toxic culture whilst inclusion without diversity can cause stagnation and limit creativity. A recent Forbes survey found that 88% of employers consider D&I initiatives essential to their success, with 80% having a positive view of their impact. What is Diversity & Inclusion?
Diversity and inclusion is ensuring different groups or individuals of different backgrounds are culturally and socially accepted and integrated into an environment or culture.
